# LMR-400 Type Coaxial Cable Comparison

All cables listed are 50-ohm, ~0.405" OD, low-loss coaxial cables in the "400-type" category.

## Specifications

| Manufacturer | Product ID | OD (in) | Velocity Factor | Center Conductor | Loss @ 30 MHz (dB/100ft) | Loss @ ~150 MHz (dB/100ft) | Direct Burial |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Times Microwave | LMR-400 | 0.405 | 85% | Solid CCA, 10 AWG | 0.7 | 1.5 | No ¹ |
| DX Engineering | DXE-400MAX | 0.405 | 84% | Stranded copper, 10 AWG, 19 strand | 0.8 | 1.8 | Yes |
| ABR Industries | ABR400-UF (25400F) | 0.405 | 84% | Stranded copper, 10 AWG, 19 strand | 0.8 | 1.8 | Yes |
| Messi & Paoloni | Airborne 10 | 0.405 | 87% | Solid CCA, Ø 2.78mm | 0.58 * | 1.28 | Yes |
| Messi & Paoloni | Extraflex Bury 10 | 0.405 | 87% | Stranded copper, 19 wire, Ø 2.9mm | 0.61 * | 1.43 | Yes |
| Davis RF | DRF-400 | 0.405 | 85% | Solid CCA, 10 AWG | 0.7 | 1.5 | Yes |

### Notes

1. Standard LMR-400 is not rated for direct burial. Times Microwave sells a separate **LMR-400-DB** variant with a fluid elastic polymer layer for direct burial applications.

\* Messi & Paoloni publish specs at 28 MHz in dB/100m. Values converted to dB/100ft (multiplied by 0.3048). Original values: Airborne 10 = 1.9 dB/100m @ 28 MHz; Extraflex Bury 10 = 2.0 dB/100m @ 28 MHz.

### Key Observations

- The **Messi & Paoloni Airborne 10** has the lowest loss at both 30 MHz and 150 MHz, along with the highest velocity factor (87%).
- **LMR-400** and **DRF-400** appear to share identical published specs — DRF-400 is marketed as a direct equivalent.
- **DXE-400MAX** and **ABR400-UF** both use stranded 19-wire pure copper center conductors, which adds flexibility but slightly increases loss vs solid CCA designs.
- **Extraflex Bury 10** shares the Hyperflex 10 electrical specs but adds a PE jacket and petrol jelly layer for direct burial and rotor use. Its stranded copper conductor provides flexibility for rotor loops.
- The **Airborne 10** achieves the lowest loss with a solid CCA conductor and is 45% lighter than comparable 10.3mm cables.
- Standard **LMR-400** is the only cable in this group not rated for direct burial without upgrading to the DB variant.
